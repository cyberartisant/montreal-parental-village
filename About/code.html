Our Commitment to Every Family in Montreal.

We are more than an organization — we are a community built on the belief that every single parent deserves support, understanding, and the chance to thrive.

Our Story

Montreal Parental Village was born out of a recognition that single parents in our city faced enormous challenges — not just financial, but emotional and social. Too many were navigating parenthood in isolation, without the network of support that makes all the difference.

We set out to change that. By bringing together single parents, community volunteers, local organizations, and advocates, we have built a village right here in Montreal — a place where no parent is left to face the journey alone.

anchor

Our Mission

Montreal Parental Village exists to support single parents by building a strong, inclusive community rooted in mutual aid, practical support, and shared responsibility. Guided by the belief that it takes a village, we work to reduce isolation, strengthen family stability, and ensure that no parent has to navigate parenting alone.

Our mission is to help single parents access the resources and community support they need to achieve financial stability and family well-being.

Our Vision

We envision a Montreal where every single-parent family has the tools, confidence, and community they need to flourish — where challenges become opportunities and every parent feels valued, understood, and empowered to succeed.
